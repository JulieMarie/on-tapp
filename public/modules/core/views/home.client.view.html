Summary

Users can locate breweries within a 10 mile radius of their current location. They can rate the beers at those specific breweries and receive recommendations of other beers that are similar.

Problem

We all love beer, and we face an unprecedented variety of options. Where to find the beers we like? What should I drink next?

Solution

OnTapp's goal is to solve these two problems. We would like to discover new and interesting beers, not simply based on categories, but also our current location and personal taste preferences.
